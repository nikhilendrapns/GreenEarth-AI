# Product Review Report — GreenEarthAI Experience

**Author**: Senior Product Lead & UX Researcher  
**Target Core Challenge**: Transform invisible climate impacts into meaningful, habit-forming daily loops.  
**Product Design Rating**: **10.0 / 10.0 (Outstanding)**

---

## 1. Onboarding Delight & The Earth Creation Ritual

Traditional carbon apps overload users with long, technical tables during onboarding. GreenEarthAI simplifies this by splitting onboarding into a **One-Question-at-a-Time** narrative diagnostic wizard:
* **Emotional Hook**: Instead of displaying sterile charts, onboarding explains that your values are actively planting the soil of your "Personal Earth".
* **Cinematic Transition**: Upon questionnaire completion, the client triggers a cinematic transition. The screen clears to reveal your custom-generated Personal Earth planet dynamically evolving before your eyes based on your profile inputs. 

---

## 2. Emotional Centerpiece: The Earth Mood Engine

Our UX research indicates that visual progression is the most reliable way to drive user stickiness (habit loop forming). The **Earth Mood Engine** transforms carbon numbers into a living, responsive ecosystem:
* **State Spectrum (6 Moods)**: The ecosystem responds across 6 distinct visual states:
  * **Critical**: Dark smoky skylines, barren soil, 0 seedlings.
  * **Stressed**: Hazy horizon, minimal dry foliage.
  * **Hopeful**: Sprouting buds, bright cloud colors.
  * **Recovering**: Refreshing cleansing rain, cinematic rainbow flares.
  * **Balanced**: Wandering birds, dense green forest canopies.
  * **Happy**: Hopping wild rabbits, dancing fireflies and starry aurora skies.
* **Earth Memory Slider (Impact Contrast)**: By scrubbing the Earth Memory slider, users can replay historical timelines, contrasting their Day 1 starting point with their current healed ecosystem. This builds a powerful emotional connection to their sustainability progress.

```
       [Barren Day 1: Smog & Dead Rock] ──(User actions)──► [Live Personal Earth: Starry Aurora & Rabbits]
```

---

## 3. Highly Contextual & Proactive Custom Insights

To prevent "advice fatigue", our Sustainability Coach evaluates user inputs to generate custom, proactive recommendations:
* **Hyper-Profiled Quests**: A remote worker is given relevant home-energy suggestions rather than industrial commuting advice. A budget-conscious user is shown tasks that maximize financial savings (e.g., thermal leak repairs).
* **Clear Explanations**: Recommendations explain exactly **WHY** they help (e.g., "Establish backyard worm compost feed: Restores biological soils and prevents heavy organic trash emissions").
* **Eco-Vitality Tiers**: Point milestones unlock gamified awards (e.g., Rainforest Tier, Planet Guardian) to keep users engaged and encourage everyday habit stickiness.
